Bart

What is Bart?

Bay Area Rapid Transit is a rapid transit public transportation system serving the San Francisco Bay Area in California. The heavy rail elevated and subway system connects San Francisco and Oakland with urban and suburban areas in Alameda, Contra Costa, and San Mateo counties.

Clipper

Clipper is the all-in-one transit card for the Bay Area. Click here for a current list of the systems that accept Clipper. Your Clipper card can hold transit passes, cash value or any combination. Cash value works on all participating transit systems. Passes and tickets are specific to each system. You can add value to your card as you go, or for added convenience, you can set up Autoload, which automatically reloads your card whenever your pass expires or your cash value balance falls below $10.
